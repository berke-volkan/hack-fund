# Hack Fund

## What is Hack Fund?
Hack Fund is a community-driven funding program designed to support the Hack Club community. Unlike traditional funding models, Hack Fund provides a zero-cost solution for contributors while ensuring maximum impact with minimal effort.

## How Does It Work?
Hack Fund enables Hack Club members to contribute effortlessly by leveraging simple, passive revenue-generating methods. Here’s how it works:

1. **Join as a Volunteer**
   - Download the Hack Fund browser extension or mobile app.
   
2. **Opt-in to Revenue Streams**
   - Choose one or more non-intrusive revenue-generating methods:
     - **Non-intrusive ads**: Displayed only when your phone is charging or as a subtle banner on websites (for browser extensions).
     - **Bandwidth sharing**: Securely share unused bandwidth through trusted APIs like HoneyGain.
     
3. **Earn Credits & Support Programs** *(Optional)*
   - Based on your contributions, earn credits that you can allocate to your preferred Hack Club programs.
   
4. **Fund Distribution**
   - At the end of each month, all generated revenue is pooled and distributed to official Hack Club accounts, based on the programs selected by users and their earned credits.

## Why Choose Hack Fund?
- **Zero cost for contributors** – No financial commitment required.
- **Effortless participation** – Generate funds passively.
- **Community-powered** – Directly supports Hack Club initiatives.
